Company description

Cairo3A For Agriculture Production is one of the Leading agriculture exporters company in the world.
Cairo 3A with a production of more own their farms in Wadi Natron and outsourcing too with over 60,0000 tons per season; Grapes, Pomegranate, Lemon, Mandarin, Navel, Navel late, Shamouti, Baladi, Valencia and different variety of vegetables.
Packed in our packhouses, and under our full supervision and control from nursery to our client's depots.
In addition to GLOBAL GAP, BRC, ISO 18001, ISO 22000, ISO 9001and being a SEDEX member, in 2014, the company added TNC, and F2F certificates. Other certificates are in plans to be added.
Therefore, professional commercial and logistics teams were employed and a big packhouse with a capacity of 100 pallets per day as instructed. product from nursery till the arrival to client's ports, first-hand handling. On the safety side, every lot of production is analyzed and the sample numbers are written on each carton label. A positive release policy is followed in the packhouses. Spray records and analysis certificates are ready and can be asked at any time.
Our Customer's base now covers more than 60 countries in the world and main markets are Europe (especially UK), Russia, Ukraine, Scandinavian countries, Fareast, Gulf countries, Baltic countries, and Africa.
